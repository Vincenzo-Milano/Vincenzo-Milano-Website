About Me

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by technology. It all started with dismantling old household devices to understand how they worked, and by the age of 13, I was building custom computers. My passion for technology grew as I explored new skills and challenges, even starting my own coding club with two friends in high school to share my interests.

This passion led me to pursue a double degree in Computer Science and Business at Wilfrid Laurier University, where I’ve had the opportunity to connect with amazing individuals from both the tech and business worlds. Throughout my journey, I’ve been involved in numerous clubs and leadership roles, and I’m now the Chief Strategy Officer of Konfer and Co-President of the Laurier Computing Society.

I’m committed to expanding my knowledge and skills every day, with a drive to innovate and make meaningful contributions to the fields I love.
